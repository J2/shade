1:Move about the stage using the arrow keys.
2:Run over mushrooms to collect them, they'll follow behind you.
3:There are four types of mushrooms in all. Each with different properties.
4:Once you've got some mushrooms return to your picnic to collect them.
5:Try to avoid running in the sunlight though, you're irish.
6:When in the sun your meter drains, when it's empty you lose.
7:You're mushrooms are irish too, it seems. Try to keep them in the shade as well.
8:Collect as many mushrooms as you can before nightfall. Good luck!
